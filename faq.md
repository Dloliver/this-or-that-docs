# ❓ FAQ

### Does the bot work in multiple servers?
Yes. Each server has its own configuration.

---

### Can I change the posting channel later?
Yes—run:


/tot channel


---

### Can it post more than once per day?
Yes—manual posting is always available via:


/tot post


---

### Why isn’t `/tot schedule` posting?
Most common causes:
- Channel not set (`/tot channel`)
- Bot lacks permissions in the channel
- Schedule wasn’t configured successfully

---

### What’s the difference between `/tot categories` and `/tot toggle`?
- `/tot categories` = full management (set/add/remove/view)
- `/tot toggle` = quick on/off for one category

---

### What does Premium add?
Custom packs, question management, and theme days:
- `/tot pack`
- `/tot question`
- `/tot theme`
