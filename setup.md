# ⚙️ Setup Guide

This guide walks you through setting up **This or That** in your Discord server.

---

## Step 1: Invite the Bot

Use the official invite link provided by the developer.

> You must have **Manage Server** permission to add bots to a server.

---

## Step 2: Set the Posting Channel (Required)

Choose where This or That questions will be posted:



/tot channel


Select a text channel from the prompt. The bot will confirm once saved.

---

## Step 3: Configure Automatic Posting (Optional)

Set when the bot posts automatically:



/tot schedule


You can configure:
- Daily posting
- Weekly posting

---

## Step 4: Post a Test Question (Recommended)

Confirm everything works:



/tot post


---

## Optional: Customize Content

Admins can fine-tune what gets posted using:
- Categories (`/tot categories`, `/tot toggle`)
- Selection behavior (`/tot mode`)

Premium servers can also use:
- Custom packs (`/tot pack`, `/tot question`)
- Theme days (`/tot theme`)
